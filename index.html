Why We Fast

Fasting isn’t about skipping meals or being extra spiritual. It’s about making space for God and removing distractions so we can hear Him more clearly. We’re not fasting to get something from God—we’re fasting to give Him our first and best.

Types of Fasts

Complete Fast

A fast from all solid foods, consuming only liquids such as water, light juices, or broths.

Selective Fast (Daniel Fast)

Removing certain foods like meat, sweets, or processed foods. Focuses on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and water.

Partial Fast

Fasting during certain times of the day, such as skipping breakfast and lunch.

Soul Fast (Everyone Is Called to This)

Stepping away from digital and emotional distractions such as social media, streaming, news, or anything that steals focus. Replace that time with prayer, Scripture, worship, and silence.

Prayer:

Day 4: Hospitality

Romans 12:13

Invite someone in or encourage them.

Day 5: Healing

Day 6: Legacy

Action:

Day 7: Light

Prayer:
