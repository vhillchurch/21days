Week One Devotional Message

A Message from Pastor Mason

Victory Hill Family,

As we wrap up our first week of 21 Days of Prayer and Fasting, I just want to pause and say this to you: I’m proud of you.

Wherever you are, you’re not behind. You’re right where God can meet you.

God is not asking you for perfection. He’s inviting you into connection.

Prayer is learned. Prayer is practiced. Prayer is proven.

Pick one time, one place, and one simple prayer. When you think to pray, pray.

Either God will move the situation, or God will move you. Both are gifts.

With you and for you,
Pastor Mason

Week Two: WORK

Jesus in what we do, not just where we go.

Day 8: Purpose

Colossians 3:23

Purpose is not just found in titles or paychecks. God can use every bit of what you do. He is present in your everyday faithfulness.

Prayer:

Help me serve You in everything I do.

Action:

Ask God to show you the purpose in your current season.

Day 9: Stewardship

1 Corinthians 4:2

God entrusts us with time, energy, and influence. Stewardship is not about doing more, but aligning what we do with what matters most.

Prayer:

Teach me to steward my time, talents, and tasks well.

Action:

Take inventory of how your time is being spent.

Day 10: Influence

Matthew 5:13

You do not need a platform to have influence. The way you speak, serve, and show up points people toward Jesus.

Prayer:

Use me to influence others for Your glory.

Action:

Encourage someone in your workplace or school today.

Day 11: Joy in the Doing

Ecclesiastes 3:12–13

Joy is not reserved for the big moments. God meets us in the ordinary and offers joy in the middle of routine.

Prayer:

Restore joy in my daily responsibilities.

Action:

Identify one thing you enjoy about your work today.

Day 12: Provision

Philippians 4:19

Provision is more than finances. God provides strength, peace, wisdom, and grace right when we need it.

Prayer:

I trust You to meet my needs.

Action:

Thank God for one recent way He has provided.

Day 13: Wisdom

James 1:5

God gives wisdom generously. You do not have to figure it all out alone.

Prayer:

Give me wisdom for today’s decisions.

Action:

Invite the Holy Spirit into one decision today.

Day 14: Rest

Exodus 20:8

Rest is not weakness. It is trust. When we rest, we declare that God is in control.
