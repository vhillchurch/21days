21 Days of Prayer & Fasting

January 7–27, 2026

Focusing on where we live, work, and play

Week 3: Where We Play

This video shares the heart behind the week. The devotionals below will help you live it out day by day.

Daily Devotionals

Week Two: WORK

Jesus in what we do, not just where we go.

Week Three: PLAY

Praying over joy, relationships, rest, and community.

Day 15: Joy

Nehemiah 8:10

Joy is not denial of difficulty. It is strength that comes from trusting God in the middle of it.

Prayer:

Help me choose joy even when it is hard.

Action:

Do one thing today that brings you joy on purpose.

Day 16: Friendship

Proverbs 27:17

God uses relationships to shape us. Life-giving friendships sharpen us and remind us we are not alone.

Prayer:

Strengthen my relationships and bring life-giving connection.

Action:

Thank or encourage a friend who helped you through a season.

Day 17: Fun

Ecclesiastes 8:15

Enjoyment is a gift from God. Faith does not remove joy from life. It restores it.

Prayer:

Teach me to enjoy the life You have given.

Action:

Set aside time for a fun or refreshing activity today.

Day 18: Presence

Psalm 46:10

Stillness helps us notice God. Slowing down creates space to hear Him more clearly.

Prayer:

Help me slow down and be fully present.

Action:

Unplug for one hour today and be fully engaged.

Day 19: Restoration

Psalm 23:3

God restores what life wears down. He refreshes our strength and renews our souls.

Prayer:

Restore what has been worn down in me.

Action:

Do something today that renews your mind, body, or spirit.

Day 20: Joy for Others

Romans 12:15

Celebrating others shifts our hearts from comparison to gratitude.

Prayer:

Teach me to celebrate with others.

Action:

Encourage someone today with a text, call, or kind word.

Day 21: Freedom

2 Corinthians 3:17

Freedom is the fruit of walking with the Spirit. What God has started, He will continue.

Prayer:

Where Your Spirit is, there is freedom.

Action:

Reflect on what God has done during these 21 days. Write it down.
