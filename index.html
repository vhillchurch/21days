21 Days of Prayer & Fasting

January 7–27, 2026

Focusing on where we live, work, and play

Week One Devotional Message

Watch this video for the heart behind Week One. The daily devotionals below will walk you through living out faith in the everyday spaces of home and community.

Why We Fast

Fasting isn’t about skipping meals or being extra spiritual. It’s about voluntarily giving something up to seek God with more focus. We’re not fasting to get something from God—we’re fasting to give Him our first and best as we begin the year.

Week One: LIVE

Jesus in the everyday spaces where we live.

Day 1: Foundation

Joshua 24:15 — “As for me and my house, we will serve the Lord.”

Let’s be honest—some days it feels like just surviving the chaos is enough. But today’s verse isn’t just a declaration—it’s a decision. When we say “as for me and my house,” we’re saying Jesus is invited into the middle of the mess.

Prayer:

Lord, be the center of my home—my space, my routine, and my relationships.

Action:

Ask: How can I honor Jesus in my home today?

Day 2: Peace

John 14:27 — “Peace I leave with you; my peace I give you.”

Jesus isn’t promising an easy life—He’s promising a steady heart. Pray peace through every room of your home today.

Prayer:

Speak peace over every room and relationship.

Action:

Walk through your house and pray over each room.

Day 3: Unity

Psalm 133:1 — “How good and pleasant it is when God’s people live together in unity.”

Prayer:

God, bring unity in our families and households.

Action:

Reach out to a family member you’ve been distant from.

Day 4: Hospitality

Romans 12:13 — “Practice hospitality.”

Prayer:

Help us open our homes and hearts to others.

Action:

Invite someone in or send a message of encouragement.

Day 5: Healing

Isaiah 61:1 — “He has sent me to bind up the brokenhearted.”

Prayer:

Heal what’s been broken in our homes.

Action:

Write a prayer for your family and keep it visible.

Day 6: Legacy

Deuteronomy 6:6–7 — “Impress them on your children.”

Prayer:

Let faith grow strong in the next generation.

Action:

Share a faith story with a child or teen in your life.

Day 7: Light

Matthew 5:14–16 — “You are the light of the world.”

Prayer:

Let our homes shine Your light in our communities.

Action:

Do something kind for a neighbor today.

Next Steps

Pick your fast. Set your prayer goals. Join us on Wednesday nights. Let’s go all in together.
